Dataset Overview:
This dataset comprises information on 5000 customers, wherein each customer is offered a loan based on their earned points. 

The dataset includes the following features:

- Age: Age of the customer in completed years
- Experience: Years of professional experience
- Income: Annual income of the customer
- Zip code: Customer's home address zip code
- Family: Size of the customer's family
- CCAvg: Average monthly spending on credit cards
- Education: Level of education (1 for Undergraduate, 2 for Graduate, 3 for Advanced)
- Mortgage: Value of the house mortgage, if applicable
- Personal_loan: Indicates if the customer accepted the personal loan offered in the last campaign (0 for No, 1 for Yes)
- Security_account: Indicates if the customer holds a securities account with the bank (0 for No, 1 for Yes)
- Cd_account: Indicates if the customer holds a certificate of deposit (CD) account with the bank (0 for No, 1 for Yes)
- Online: Indicates if the customer uses internet banking facilities (0 for No, 1 for Yes)
- Creditcard: Indicates if the customer uses a credit card issued by Universal Bank (0 for No, 1 for Yes)
